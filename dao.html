Si Her FAQ

Quick answers about Si Her DAO — who we are, how we govern, and how to join.

What is Si Her DAO?

Si Her is a leadership collaborative of womxn in emerging tech governed by its DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization).

Who is in Si Her DAO?

Founders, operators, researchers, and contributors from 50+ womxn-led communities across Web3, AI, and emerging tech — plus partner ecosystems and aligned industry leaders.

Does the DAO have a token?

Membership and contribution are signaled on-chain via NFTs and reputation credentials issued through Unlock Protocol. A fungible governance token is not part of the current design.

How do I join the DAO, and is there a fee?

Apply via the “Join our DAO” flow above. New members are onboarded through an aligned community lead and a Si Her DAO orientation; there is no membership fee for individuals.
